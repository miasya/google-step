Lifeng's Stir-Fried Rice

Simple but flavourful dish to bring to any potluck. This dish takes a spin on the classic Chinese Stir-Fried rice by incorporating Canadian ingredients and flavours. We always prepare this dish for family gatherings because it's so good!

Ingredients

  • Cooked Ham
  • Rice, room temperature/cold
  • Peas
  • Corn
  • Carrots, chopped
  • Salt

Steps

  • Heat a pan on high and add cooking oil
  • Add all incredients
  • Remove from heat when cooked
  • Serve warm
