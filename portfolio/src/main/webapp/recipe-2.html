Nanny's Shortbread Cookies

Melt-in-your-mouth delicious cookies. We would make dozens and dozens of these throughout the holidays. Fun to decorate and the perfect gift for friends!

Ingredients

  • Softened Butter
  • Rice Flour
  • White Sugar
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  • Mix ingredients in a large bowl
  • Drop cookies onto pan
  • Decorate
  • Bake at 375C until the bottoms turn brown (warning, they cook quick!)
  • Nom nom nom!
