Friend Tom Nitio's Eggrolls

Tom Nitio's family was one of nanny and grandpa's first family friends when they made their move to Ottawa. Every summer, our family dedicates an entire day to make 100+ of these delicious eggrolls to enjoy. Can be frozen for long periods of time, and kept to satify cravings whenever desired!

Ingredients (100 eggrolls)

  • 2:1 ratio of ground pork to lean ground pork
  • 1 head finely chopped round cabbage, remove excess juice
  • 1 can waterchestnuts, drained and chopped
  • 1 can bamboo shoots, drained and chopped
  • 2 packs eggroll wraps
  • Bean sprouts
  • Salt, Bean Paste, Hoisin Sauce

Steps

  • Mix ingredients in a large bowl
  • Wrap a spoonful into each eggroll wrapper
  • Press the ends of the eggroll down with a fork
  • Deep fry until golden
  • Enjoy!
